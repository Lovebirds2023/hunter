Lovedogs 360 Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your Lovedogs 360 account and associated personal data.

Delete Your Account In The App

  1. Open the Lovedogs 360 app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Scroll to Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompts.

After confirmation, your account is deleted and you are signed out.

If You Cannot Access The App

Send an account deletion request to admin@lovedogs360.com using the email address linked to your Lovedogs 360 account.

You can also use the in-app Support screen if you can still sign in.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your login access and authentication links are removed.
  • Your name, email, phone number, profile image, bio, location, payout contact details, and push token are removed.
  • Your pet records, health records, private messages, support tickets, saved events, and personal community content are removed where possible.
  • Some transaction, payment, audit, or safety records may be retained without your personal identity where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, or community safety reasons.

This action cannot be undone.
