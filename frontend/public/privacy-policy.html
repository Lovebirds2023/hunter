Lovedogs 360 Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

App: Lovedogs 360 | Contact: admin@lovedogs360.com

Lovedogs 360 is a pet care, marketplace, events, safety reporting, and community coordination platform. This policy explains how Lovedogs 360 collects, uses, shares, protects, retains, and deletes information when you use the Lovedogs 360 mobile app, website, and related services.

We do not sell personal data. We do not store card numbers, CVV codes, or card expiry dates on our servers. Card payments are handled through Pesapal's hosted payment flow.

1. Information We Collect

Account and profile information

  • Name, email address, password hash, authentication provider, Google account identifier if you use Google sign-in, role, country code, preferred language, profile photo, bio, and account status.
  • Phone number or WhatsApp number if you provide it for contact, marketplace coordination, event registration, or payout setup.

Location information

  • Approximate or precise foreground location, location accuracy, coordinates, addresses, and landmarks when you allow location access.
  • Location may be used for nearby marketplace listings, events, lost and found reports, case reports, service discovery, safety alerts, and proximity features.
  • We do not request background location access. Location is collected when the app feature needs it and you grant permission.

Pet and animal identity information

  • Pet name, species, breed, color, age, size, sex, weight, height, bio, unique markings, pet care records, vaccination information, and related notes.
  • Pet photos, nose print images, body images, birthmark images, vaccination card images, and animal identity descriptors used to help identify or match pets.
  • Animal identity data is used for pet identification and lost/found matching. We do not collect human faceprints, fingerprints, voiceprints, or other human biometric identifiers.

Marketplace, events, and payment information

  • Listings, service and product details, prices, stock or slots, bookings, event registrations, ticket data, QR/check-in records, form answers, receipts, order status, payout records, commission records, and transaction references.
  • Payout preference and M-Pesa phone number if you configure payouts.
  • Pesapal payment tracking IDs, merchant references, payment status, and related receipt information. Card details are entered with Pesapal and are not stored by Lovedogs 360.

Community, safety, support, and communications

  • Case reports, lost/found reports, photos, descriptions, comments, likes, flags, community posts, polls, reactions, private messages, support tickets, support images, and admin replies.
  • Content you choose to post publicly may be visible to other users. Direct messages and support tickets are private to the intended participants and authorized administrators, except where review is needed for safety, moderation, legal, or abuse-prevention reasons.

Program, wellbeing, and scorecard information

  • If you join a Lovedogs 360 program, event, or scorecard survey, we may collect attendance, check-ins, participant role, county or community location, consent status, survey answers, wellbeing and stress-scale responses, relationship responses, animal welfare snapshots, observations, and related program evidence.
  • Program reporting may use aggregated or de-identified results where possible.

Device, app, and diagnostic information

  • Push notification token, app version, language preference, authentication tokens stored on your device, sync queue data for offline event work, audit logs, timestamps, IP address and request metadata, and basic diagnostic data needed to secure and operate the service.
  • Camera, photo library, notification, and location permissions are requested only for features that need them.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create, authenticate, maintain, and secure your account.
  • Provide pet identity, pet care passport, lost/found, case reporting, marketplace, event, community, messaging, support, and seller payout features.
  • Show nearby services, products, events, reports, and safety information when location-based features are used.
  • Process bookings, event registrations, payments, receipts, refunds, commissions, and payouts.
  • Send service messages, support replies, safety updates, event information, and app notifications where permitted.
  • Moderate content, investigate reports, prevent fraud or abuse, enforce platform rules, and protect users, animals, providers, and the community.
  • Improve app reliability, troubleshoot issues, maintain audit logs, and comply with legal, tax, accounting, payment, and safety obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to provide the service, follow your choices, operate safely, or comply with law.

  • Other users: Public posts, marketplace listings, event details, approved case reports, community content, ratings, and profile details you choose to display may be visible to other users.
  • Providers, buyers, and event organizers: Booking, registration, form-answer, ticket, and order information may be shared with the relevant provider, buyer, organizer, facilitator, or admin. Phone numbers are shared only when the app feature or your choice allows it.
  • Administrators and moderators: Authorized admins may review support tickets, flagged content, reports, payment records, event records, program data, and user records to operate and protect the platform.
  • Service providers: We use vendors that help run the app, including hosting, database, file storage, authentication, maps, notifications, email, payment processing, and app distribution services. These may include Railway, Supabase, Vercel, Google services, Expo services, Resend or SMTP email providers, and Pesapal.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, payment rules, fraud prevention, security investigations, or urgent safety needs.
  • Business transfers: If Lovedogs 360 is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this policy or a policy with comparable protections.

4. Payments

Lovedogs 360 uses Pesapal for hosted payment processing. When you pay by card or another supported payment method, Pesapal collects and processes payment credentials under its own security controls. Lovedogs 360 stores payment status, amounts, transaction references, receipts, order records, payout records, and related metadata needed to complete the transaction, support users, prevent fraud, and satisfy accounting obligations.

Lovedogs 360 is not a bank, lending app, investment app, cryptocurrency service, stored-value wallet, or money transfer service. Seller payout information is used only to pay marketplace sellers for completed Lovedogs 360 orders.

5. Pet Care and Impact Assessment Records

Lovedogs 360 stores pet care records and helps users coordinate with independent pet-care professionals. The app does not provide diagnosis, prescriptions, emergency care, or treatment for people or pets. Always consult a qualified veterinarian directly for pet care decisions.

Some Lovedogs 360 programs may include consent-based impact assessments, wellbeing or stress-scale questions, follow-up surveys, observations, and evidence uploads for monitoring, evaluation, research, and partner reporting. These features are used to understand program outcomes and are not a replacement for professional medical, mental-health, veterinary, or emergency services.

6. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect data, including HTTPS connections, password hashing, secure token storage on supported devices, access controls, role-based admin permissions, and provider security controls for storage, hosting, payments, and authentication. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so please use a strong password and protect access to your device and email account.

7. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain your account, resolve disputes, enforce rules, prevent fraud, support safety investigations, comply with legal and accounting requirements, and maintain backups.

  • Account, profile, pet, support, message, and user content data is generally kept while your account is active.
  • Transaction, payment, payout, tax, accounting, audit, moderation, safety, and fraud-prevention records may be kept longer where required or reasonably necessary.
  • Backups and logs may take additional time to expire from storage systems.
  • Public or community content may be removed, anonymized, or retained in limited form if needed for community safety, records integrity, legal compliance, or abuse prevention.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update your profile, language, phone, bio, payout preference, and other account details in the app.
  • You can control device permissions such as location, camera, photos, and notifications from your device settings. Some features may not work without the required permission.
  • You can choose whether to share your phone number for certain marketplace and event flows where the app provides that choice.
  • You can request access, correction, deletion, or help with privacy questions by contacting admin@lovedogs360.com.

9. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your Lovedogs 360 account in the app:

  • Open Lovedogs 360.
  • Go to Profile.
  • Open Account Settings.
  • Tap Delete Account and confirm the prompts.

If you cannot access the app, email admin@lovedogs360.com from the email address connected to your account. You can also review our account deletion instructions at https://hunter-k9lr.vercel.app/account-deletion.html.

After account deletion, we delete or de-identify personal account data where possible. Some transaction, audit, safety, fraud-prevention, accounting, legal, or backup records may be retained as described in this policy.

10. Children's Privacy

Lovedogs 360 is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate permission, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

11. International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries where Lovedogs 360, our hosting providers, payment providers, and other service providers operate. Data protection rules may differ by country, but we use this policy and provider safeguards to protect information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and may provide notice in the app, by email, or through the website.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, account deletion, or data requests, contact Lovedogs 360 at admin@lovedogs360.com.
